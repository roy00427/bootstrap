LUCENT

Solution to Pollution

Lucent is now, India's largest and most influential grassroots environmental organization, working mainly to control the air pollution in and around the national capital which effect the weather conditions. There are one million members and supporters working together to improve the air quality and reduce the deadly smog which is being experienced since last few years. Our success range from protecting acres of wilderness to helping pass the Clean Air Act. Lucent is investing in developing pollution forecasting apps to make a better future and prevent climate change.
